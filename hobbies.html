MY HOBBIES

Baseball

I have been playing baseball for as long as I can remember. My dad was a pitcher in college, and many others in my family have played as well. Baseball is what I commit most of my time out of school towards, and I'm looking to play in college. I pitch and play infield, and next year our school team can make the playoffs.



Academic UIL

I was recently recruited by our school's Calculus BC teacher to participate in Academic UIL, where I competed in the calculator apps division. The competition entails receiving a 70 question math test in which competitors race to enter the questions in to their calculator. It is possible to get a negative score, but thankfully I did well enough to place in the top 10 in my first competition despite it being my first one.
